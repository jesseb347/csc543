Project 1: Understanding Cybersecurity Threats in Social Media

Summary

As social media usage intensifies, so does the landscape of cybersecurity threats associated with it. The research will explore the complexities of this relationship, focusing on identifying the vulnerabilities and threats that social media platforms pose to individuals and organizations.

Triple-Modular Strategy

The project aims to highlight the importance of cybersecurity in social media by visualizing data related to various cybersecurity threats. Using a triple-modular strategy, the project demonstrates the consistent representation of cybersecurity data across three different front-end implementations, ensuring reliability and redundancy in data presentation.
